FAQs

Does each team have to compete in all 5 events??

Yes. Every team must register to compete in all 5 events

Will laptops/systems be provided?

Only for network communications and the app build pitch. You must use your own device for all other event challenges.

Can teams use a Mac?

Yes teams can use a Mac. Please ensure it has an updated web browser. If using the Mac to perform the Server Hardening event, please ensure it has the ability to RDP to a Windows device.

Can teams meet at CoWerx46 or ASU to compete in a virtual event challenge?

Yes, there are rooms, space and internet access available at both locations to access and work on your event challenge at either of these spaces.

Will there be food and drinks available at CoWerx46 and/or ASU?

Yes, there will be complementary snacks and drinks available at CoWerx throughout the competition beginning on Thursday evening for teams as they collaborate.

Are participants allowed to use Google, mentors, teachers, etc to compete in an event challenge?

Yes. Teams are encouraged to use multiple sources.

When do participants need to wear their name badges?

Name badges must be worn while in CoWerx, ASU or the Renaissance while collaborating or competing in an event challenge.

Does each team member have to participate in every event?

No.

Will we need to provide our own equipment for each event challenge?

You will need to provide your own system for Website Attack and for all virtual events.

Will Smart City/Smart Base/Smart Campus problems be provided?

No, teams are responsible for presenting problems and solutions to the panel of judges during their pitch on Monday evening.

Will the Internet be accessible during the app pitch competition?

No. All presentations submitted must not have embedded links to the internet.

Can non-team members attend or participate in event challenges?

No. Non-team members cannot participate in event challenges. The application pitch event challenge is open to the public for others to attend.

What is the attire for all event challenges?

Participants are welcome to wear casual attire (no inappropriate language) at a minimum.
